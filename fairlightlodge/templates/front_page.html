Fairlight Lodge B&B combines the charm of a Victorian home with all the comforts of modern living. Our location, within walking distance of King’s Lynn restaurants, shops and historic town centre and not far from Sandringham and the stunning Norfolk coast, makes us an ideal place to stay when visiting for leisure or business. Also we are adjacent to ‘The Walks‘, the green heart of King’s Lynn

Our emphasis is on friendliness and a relaxed atmosphere. The six en suite rooms and one family room are all well appointed, with Freeview TV, free WiFi and home made biscuits.

Our extensive breakfast menu caters for all tastes, and is served fresh to order. Off road parking and secure bicycle storage is also available.
